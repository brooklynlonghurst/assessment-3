My Neighborhood

Pleasant Grove, UT.

About Pleasant Grove:

History
  • Settled in 1850
  • Orginally named Battle Creek
  • It's known as "Utah's City of Trees"
Fun Facts
  • There's a 'G' on Little Mountain that stands for "the Grove".
  • There have been large lights on Little Mountain of a star for Christmas and a heart for Valentine's Day.
  • There is a summer festival called Strawberry Days that includes rodeos, parades, carnivals and pageants.
